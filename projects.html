What we do

Mathematics. Don't run away! We aren't going to bore you with abstract and unintelligible formulas. There's enough of that in highschool. Mathematics is often taught in a way which makes it appear boring and useless, but it can sometimes be quite the opposite. We aim to help people without a technical background apply concepts and techniques from mathematics and computer science in accessible, practical ways.
